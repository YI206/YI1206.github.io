Regional Integrated
Healthcare Operation System
(RIHOS)

RIHOS is an interconnected and intelligent healthcare ecosystem.

It provides medical solutions that modernize healthcare from hospitals to rural/community clinics, ensuring equitable access to services. This system integrates big medical data, smart healthcare technologies, blockchain technology and advanced decision support tools to facilitate the exchange, sharing, and efficient collaboration of medical information across various healthcare facilities. The system addresses data fragmentation issue to strengthen medical informatization. With decision support tools, the system also optimizes the operations and management of the entire healthcare system, including patient flow management, resource allocation, and scheduling, to minimize delays and reduce redundancies. The system further connects the national medical center with regional centers and primary care units, provides a platform for an integrated medical collaborative care.

The system streamlines the distribution of medical resources, ensuring that all levels of healthcare facilities are adequately equipped and staffed, leading to improved patient outcomes and increased satisfaction.

KEY TECHNOLOGY EDGES
1. Medical Information Technology
We leverage technologies like big data, cloud computing to strengthen medical informatization, improve data management.
2. Decision support tools
We integrate AI-based decision analytics, including machine learning and optimization, to address the resource allocation challenges.
3. Collaborative Healthcare Communities
We use the IOT and AI to construct a new patient service that combines online and offline to form an integrated ecosystem.
